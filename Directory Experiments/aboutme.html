About Me

Hello students. I'm Oscar Schindler and I teach ICS 31 here at UC Irvine. For the past few years, I have been with the school of ICS studying how to make an efficient and engaging computer science course for elementary and middle school students. My research has progressed nicely and I hope to present my results sometime soon at the upcoming CS Education Conference.

Besides research, I also work part-time at Blizzard. I'm involved with server-side management. As a result, I also teach CS 122A and CS 122B, so if you are thinking about specializing in networking, you'll see me sometime in the next few years. I also help some of the other CGS and Informatics professors with their research endeavors.

My office hours are Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 2:00 to 5:00pm. Feel free to drop in. My advice to any visitors would be to write down any questions you may have about the course material. That way, we can make the most out of your time inside my office.
